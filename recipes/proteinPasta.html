Protein Pasta

Description:

This is a high protein recipe perfect for your weight loss and muscle growth goals. It's approximately 500 calories with 120g of protein. It's been a staple on my aggressive cut of 1400 calories and will keep you satiated. It's super quick and easy with easy and you can put it into containers for meal prep. You can adjust by using different ingredients types like the protein, cheese, and sauce to your liking, but hopefully still hitting your macros.

Ingredients:

  • Protein or Keto Pasta
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Half a white onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Fat free mozzarella
  • 0 Calorie oil spray

Steps:

  1. Mince up the garlic and chop up the onion into small pieces. Put the onions into a pan with some oil spray and cook until soft and golden. Then add the garlic and cook till fragrant.
  2. While that's happening boil your protein pasta according to package instructions and save some pasta water. Put your canned tomatoes in with the onion and garlic and crush up the tomatoes if they're not already
  3. Now strain your pasta and put into the sauce and add pasta water as needed until the sauce comes together. Now plate or put into a meal prep container, sprinkle the fat free mozz and enjoy!
